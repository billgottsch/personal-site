About Me

My name is Bill Gottsch and I'm a front-end engineer.

I love working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and React Native, but I'm always learning. I love working with people who are passionate about their work and their lives.


When I'm not coding I enjoy golfing, hunting, fishing, camping, hiking, gaming, watching movies and talking about all those things!

Projects

These are a few of the projects I've been working on:

Bridges Career Academies & Workplace Connection

My current capstone project built in React Native that encapsulates everything I've learned so far at The Iron Yard, as well as, things I've had to learn on my own along the way.

Revolutionz

This is a crossover project given to the Front and Back End classes (first of it's kind at The Iron Yard - Minneapolis!) where we create our own API, products and design while collaborating together to build a fun, functional and clean experience for our clients.

Novus Tacos

Our first co-op assignment (with Jenessa White, student at TIY) as a webpage for a fictional restaurant. The page is majority HTML/CSS, with a few Javascript pieces as well. We went with a bright and vibrant page to show off the beautiful food at the restaurant.

Netflix N Chill

The project was a fun, cooperative assignment made to create a simpler way for couples to decide on a date-night. The application randomly generates a movie and beverage (alcoholic and non-alcoholic), gives information and directions for creating. It was done over the course of a weekend.
